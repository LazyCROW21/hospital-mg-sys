Hospital Facilities

List of facilities available at The General Hospital

24-Hours Services

  • Emergency
  • Laboratory
  • Radiology and Imaging
  • Ambulance

24 x 7 Intensive Care facilities ( recognized by ISCCM for DCCM ) with separate isolation rooms for infectious patients, surgical intensive care, separate high dependency units, post operative care ward which opens seamlessly from the operation theatre complex

Ambulance Services:

24 hours patient transport vehicle available. Patients are transported from home to GRMI hospital and patients referred by emergency duty doctor to empanelled hospital for specialized care.

OPD

Patients are registered at the OPD Counter and are seen on first come, first serve basis, however out of turn consultation may be provided in case of emergency and senior citizen. Patients have the right to consult any doctor.

In OPD, clinical consultation is provided to patients which includes history taking, clinical examination, diagnosis and providing prescription to patients besides advising laboratory tests in some cases.

OPD has a waiting hall with chairs, a TV, public utilities like drinking water and toilets. Sub waiting areas are available in front of individual consultation rooms. Wheel chairs, trolleys and attendants are there to help very sick patients.
